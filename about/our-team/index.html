Founder & Teacher
Ustāth Nuradin Aliy

Coming from a wide variety of backgrounds and experiences, Ust. Nuradin is the backbone of Al-Madrasatu An-Nuriyyah. His ideas help shape the direction and mission of AMAN as it continues to grow and develop, insha'Allah.

Teaching Qualities
Deliberative
Thinks carefully before acting — every decision about your child's learning is thoughtful and intentional, never rushed.
Relator
Builds genuine, deep relationships — gets to know each student personally so they feel seen, valued, and understood.
Harmony
Creates a peaceful, cooperative classroom — minimizes conflict and ensures every student feels safe and respected.
Responsibility
Takes full ownership of student progress — if a child is struggling, it's taken seriously and addressed with care.
Consistency
Treats every student fairly and equally — the same high standards, rules, and respect apply to every child in the room.
