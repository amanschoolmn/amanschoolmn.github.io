Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Al-Madrasatu An-Nuriyyah ("AMAN," "we," "us") operates this website to share information about our school and to accept student applications. This page explains what information we collect and how it's used.

Information We Collect

When you submit the Student Application Form or Amaanah program form, we collect the information you provide directly, which may include: parent/guardian name and contact information, student name and date of birth, home address, emergency contact information, academic background, health information and allergies relevant to your child's safety, and a digital signature.

How We Use Information

Information submitted through our forms is used only to process enrollment applications, communicate with families about admissions and school activities, and maintain accurate student records. We do not sell or rent family information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our application forms are processed through Formspree, a third-party form service, which delivers submissions to AMAN's email inbox. This site also loads fonts from Google Fonts. These providers may process limited technical data (such as IP address) as part of delivering their service.

Photo & Video Consent

Photo and video consent is collected as its own, separate checkbox during enrollment — distinct from the liability waiver. Media is only used publicly (website, social media, promotional materials) for families who have explicitly agreed to this consent.

Children's Information

Because AMAN serves students as young as 6, some information we collect relates to minors. This information is provided by a parent or guardian, not the child directly, and is used solely for enrollment, safety, and school administration purposes.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be directed to amanschool.mn@gmail.com.

Terms of Use

By using this website, you agree to use it only for its intended purpose — learning about AMAN and applying for enrollment. Content on this site, including text, logos, and images, belongs to Al-Madrasatu An-Nuriyyah and may not be reproduced without permission.

Submitting an application through this website does not guarantee acceptance into AMAN. Enrollment decisions are made according to the admissions process described on our Admissions page.

This website is provided as-is. While we work to keep information accurate and current, AMAN is not liable for any errors or for actions taken based on information found on this site — if anything is unclear, please contact us directly.
