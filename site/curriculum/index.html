How We Teach

Structured for Every Child's Growth

At AMAN, students are grouped by skill level — not age. Every child is assessed and placed in the right environment. Group leaders support beginners, and our rotating focus ensures well-rounded development week after week.

🌱
Beginner مُبْتَدِئ
Foundation Level · Green Book
مُعَلِّمُ الْقِرَاءَةِ الْعَرَبِيَّةِ مَعَ قَاعِدَةٍ بَغْدَادِيَّة
  • Arabic alphabet recognition and writing
  • Introductory Qur'an reading
  • Basic daily supplications and adhkār
  • Islamic manners and identity
📖
Reading مُتَوَسِّط
Qur'an Reading Level
  • Reading directly from the Muṣḥaf with the eyes
  • Strengthening fluency, accuracy, and speed of recitation
  • Mastering Juz ʻAmma, Juz Tabārak, and Juz Qad Samiʻ
  • Building until the tongue flows easily and correctly
Students placed here have completed the Green Book but need more time to strengthen their recitation before advancing to Ḥifḥ.
⭐
Ḥifḥ مُتَقَدِّم
Memorization Level
  • Full Qur'an memorization (Ḥifḥ) program
  • Fluent recitation with complete tajwīd
  • Multiple juz memorized and reviewed regularly
  • Leadership and mentoring responsibilities in class
🔄
Weekly Evaluations تَقْيِيمٌ أُسْبُوعِيّ
Students are assessed weekly and advance when ready — not by age or grade. Every child moves at the pace that is right for them.
