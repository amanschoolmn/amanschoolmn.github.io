Al-Madrasatu An-Nuriyyah — Policy Guidelines

السَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكُمْ وَرَحْمَةُ اللهِ وَبَرَكَاتُهُ


We are honored to welcome you and your children to AMAN, where we strive to deliver quality Islamic education. Established in 2020, AMAN was founded to uplift our youth through innovation, bold actions, and strong community support.


  • Attendance: Unexcused absences beyond 3 days will lead to dismissal.
  • Dress Code: Islamic dress is mandatory every session.
  • Behavior: Disruptive behavior and disrespect will not be tolerated.
  • Homework: Must be submitted on time. Parents are expected to support students.
  • Preparedness: Students must bring all required materials each week.

Together, we are planting seeds of īmān, adab, and knowledge that will benefit generations to come, insha'Allah. Jazākum Allāhu Khayran for being part of the AMAN family.
