The Meaning
AMAN (أَمَان) means safety, peace, and security — the protected environment where light is nurtured. A child cannot carry light into the world without first being raised in a place of safety. That is what AMAN is.

Al-Madrasatu An-Nuriyyah is a weekend Islamic school dedicated to nurturing the next generation of Muslims through authentic Islamic education, strong character development, and meaningful connections with the Qur'an and Sunnah.

At AMAN, we believe that Islamic education is not just about memorization — it is about transforming hearts, building identity, strengthening families, and preparing young Muslims to live confidently upon Islam in today's world.

Why Families Choose AMAN

Built Around Your Child

Skill-Based, Not Age-Based
  • Students are placed by actual reading and memorization level, not just birth year
  • Weekly evaluations track real progress in every class
  • Three clear levels: foundational, reading directly from the Muṣḥaf, and Ḥifḥ memorization
A True Parent Partnership
  • Parents are treated as partners in the child's growth, not bystanders dropping off a student
  • Direct lines to the teacher and enrollment team — not a call center
  • A mandatory parent interview before enrollment, so expectations are clear from day one
