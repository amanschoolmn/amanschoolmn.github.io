The Trust
Every child is an amaanah from Allah ﷻ — a sacred trust we are responsible to protect.

Al-Madrasatu An-Nuriyyah — "The School of Light" — was founded in 2020 with a simple conviction: that raising a Muslim child is one of the greatest amaanahs (trusts) a community can be given. The name AMAN means peace and safety, and that is exactly what we set out to build — a place where children are safe to learn, to question, and to grow.

From that same foundation grew The Amaanah — our summer programs for older youth. As our students matured, we saw the need to keep walking with them: to protect the trust through the harder teenage years, when faith, identity, and character are truly tested. The brothers and sisters programs exist to honor that same trust, at a new stage of life.

Whether a six-year-old learning their first letters or a teenager finding their footing, the mission is one and the same: to take the light of revelation and help the next generation carry it — into their homes, their families, and the world.

How We Teach

Structured for Every Child's Growth

At AMAN, students are grouped by skill level — not age. Every child is assessed and placed in the right environment. Group leaders support beginners, and our rotating focus ensures well-rounded development week after week.

🌱
Beginner مُبْتَدِئ
Foundation Level
  • Arabic alphabet recognition and writing
  • Introductory Qur'an reading
  • Basic daily supplications and adhkār
  • Islamic manners and identity
📈
Intermediate مُتَوَسِّط
Building Level
  • Tajwīd rules and Qur'an recitation
  • Qur'an memorization (Juz Amma)
  • Arabic reading and vocabulary
  • Tawḥīd, Hadith, and Islamic studies
⭐
Advanced مُتَقَدِّم
Excellence Level
  • Extended Ḥifẓ — multiple juz memorization
  • Fluent Qur'an recitation with full tajwīd
  • Deeper Aqeedah and Fiqh study
  • Leadership responsibilities in class
🔄
Weekly Evaluations تَقْيِيمٌ أُسْبُوعِيّ
Students are assessed weekly and advance when ready — not by age or grade. Every child moves at the pace that is right for them.

Official Process

AMAN Admissions Process

Submitting an application does not guarantee acceptance into Al-Madrasatu An-Nuriyyah. Enrollment is based on classroom availability, student readiness, and overall program fit. Below is our full admissions process.

1
📋 Submit Student Application
Parents complete the AMAN Student Application Form with all required information.

Submitting an application does not guarantee acceptance into the program.

2
🔍 Application Review
The school reviews the application to:
  • Confirm information is complete
  • Review student age and class availability
  • Determine whether the family may be a good fit for AMAN

Families may be contacted if additional information is needed.

3
⏳ Waiting List (If Classes Are Full)
If there is no space available, the student may be placed on the AMAN waiting list. Families will be contacted if an opening becomes available, in shā Allah.

Being on the waiting list does not guarantee enrollment.

4
🤝 Mandatory Parent Interview
Before acceptance, parents must attend a Mandatory In-Person Parent Interview. During this meeting:
  • AMAN policies and expectations are explained
  • The school structure and classroom environment are discussed
  • Parents may ask questions about the program

This meeting also helps ensure good cooperation between the school and family.

5
✍️ Parent Agreement Form
Parents must complete and sign the AMAN Parent Agreement Form, confirming understanding of:
  • Attendance expectations
  • Behavior policies
  • Parent involvement
  • Communication procedures
  • Tuition policies and overall school expectations

Trial / Adjustment Period: The first few weeks of enrollment serve as a trial period to ensure the student adjusts well, classroom expectations are followed, and there is healthy cooperation between home and school. AMAN reserves the right to discontinue enrollment if the program is not a good fit for the student or family.

6
📬 Acceptance Decision
After completing the admissions steps, families will receive one of the following decisions:
✅
Accepted
Student is approved for enrollment.
⏳
Waiting List
No space available at this time — family will be contacted if an opening becomes available.
❌
Not Accepted at This Time
AMAN may determine the program is not currently the best fit.
7
🎉 Enrollment Confirmation & Class Placement
Accepted families will receive their full enrollment package, which includes:
  • Official start date
  • Class level placement — Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced — based on Qur'an reading, Arabic foundation, and overall readiness
  • Tuition and payment information
  • Required supply list
  • School communication details

Level placement is determined by the school after acceptance — ensuring every student starts in the right environment for their growth.

⚠️
Important Notice: Submitting an application does not guarantee acceptance into Al-Madrasatu An-Nuriyyah (AMAN). Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age for enrollment? ▼

Students must be at least 6 years old to enroll at AMAN.

What days and times are classes held? ▼

Classes are held on weekends from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Contact us for the current schedule day.

Does my child need prior Islamic education? ▼

No prior experience is required. We have a Beginner level for students just starting out. Every student is assessed and placed in the appropriate group — skill-based, not age-based.

Can I enroll multiple children? ▼

Yes! Our enrollment form allows you to register as many children as needed. Each student has their own section. Use the "Add Another Student" button to add more.

What is the attendance and tardiness policy? ▼

Students must arrive on time. Tardiness of more than 15 minutes results in being sent home. Unexcused absences beyond 3 days will lead to dismissal. Please notify staff by Friday of any upcoming absence.

When are fees due and how are they paid? ▼

Registration fee is $50 per student, due upon enrollment — no exceptions. Monthly tuition is $90/student, due at the beginning of every month. Payments via CashApp or Zelle at (952) 217-3629.

Is there a sibling discount? ▼

There is no sibling discount at this time. Each student is enrolled at the standard rate of $50 registration and $90/month. We appreciate your understanding as we continue to grow, insha'Allah.

What should my child wear to class? ▼

Islamic dress is mandatory every session — proper hijab for sisters and thobe or appropriate Islamic dress for brothers. Students who do not meet the dress code may be asked to return home.

What happens if my child misses a class? ▼

Please notify staff by Friday of any upcoming absence. Students are allowed a maximum of 3 unexcused absences — beyond that, enrollment may be discontinued. It is the parent's responsibility to help their child make up any missed material at home.
